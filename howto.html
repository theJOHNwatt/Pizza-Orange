How To Make Orange Chicken Pizza

    Ingredients:

  • 1 Classic Pillsbury Pizza Crust
  • 2 cups cooked chicken
  • Â½ cup orange chicken sauce
  • 1-11oz. can mandarin oranges
  • pinch crushed red pepper flakes
  • steamed broccoli

    Directions

  1. Unwrap pizza dough and form into two circles
  2. Mix chicken with orange chicken sauce and add crushed red pepper if desired.
  3. Place orange chicken on pizza.
  4. Add pieces of steamed broccoli.
  5. bake at 425 degrees for 20-25 minutes, until crust if firm.
  6. Remove from oven and top with Mandarin Oranges then serve.
